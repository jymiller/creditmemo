Credit Memo Refinery

From Dormant Archive to Proprietary Data Moat

A virtual hackathon experiment: solve a real business use case, have fun, and maybe learn a thing or two along the way.

Every commercial bank sits on decades of credit memos containing proprietary information no competitor has — your borrowers, your losses, your analysts' judgments. Almost none of it is queryable. This document set makes the business case for activating that dormant asset, shows the architecture that makes it possible, and scopes a concrete weekend demo that proves the critical link.

Suggested reading order

For executives and stakeholders: start with the One-Page Brief. If they want more, follow the link at the bottom to the Strategic Brief.

For engineers evaluating feasibility: start with the Strategic Brief's technical foundation section, then jump to the Demo Requirements Contract (sections 1–9).

For weekend hackathon participants: read the One-Page Brief for context, skim the Strategic Brief for the bigger picture, then work from the Demo Requirements — specifically The Contract — as the working spec.
